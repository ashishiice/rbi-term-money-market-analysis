⚖️Regulatory Context & Announcement

🔔 RBI Notification Summary (April 9, 2026)

Key Change: RBI has broadened term money market participation beyond banks and standalone primary dealers (PDs) to include:

  • All India Financial Institutions (AIFIs) - NABARD, NHB, EXIM Bank, SIDBI
  • Non-Banking Financial Companies (NBFCs) - including Hero FinCorp
  • Housing Finance Companies (HFCs)
  • Corporates

Additional Measure: Standalone primary dealer borrowing caps raised to improve market-making depth in the term money market.

RBI Statement: "This measure will strengthen monetary policy transmission by linking overnight MIBOR with longer-term market rates and improve price discovery in the term funding segment."
